What is the exact ratio of transportation time to rideshare time? Public transit trip time to rideshare trip time in the rest of our analysis. We call this ratio of transit time / rideshare time "Transit Penalty Score". This "Transit Penalty Score" captures how much more time it would take to complete a trip using public transportation instead of rideshare. Next we have the distributon of "Transit Penalty Scores" across our dataset. Marked in the graph is the median "Transit Penalty Score": 2.  This score means for that rideshare trip, taking public transportation would have taken 2x as long.
