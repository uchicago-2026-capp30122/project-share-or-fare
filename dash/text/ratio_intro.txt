
Using our dataset of rideshare trips, we want to understand what the potential transit alternatives were for those trips. We compare rideshare trip time with the corresponding public transit alternative time. In the following visualization there is a diagonal line representing a 1:1 ratio of transit time to rideshare time-- the public transportation time for that trip takes the same amount of time as the rideshare trip. From the following visualization we can see that for the majority of rides are above the diagonal line, meaning that for most of the rideshare trips that people are taking, the corresponding transit time is longer than the rideshare time.
