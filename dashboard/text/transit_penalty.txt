What is the exact ratio of transportation time to rideshare time? Public transit trip time to rideshare trip time in the rest of our analysis. We call this ratio of transit time / rideshare time **Transit Penalty Score**. This Transit Penalty Score captures how much more time it would take to complete a trip using public transportation instead of rideshare. You can see in the following graph that the median transit penalty score is 2.0. This score means for that rideshare trip, taking public transportation would have taken 2x as long.
