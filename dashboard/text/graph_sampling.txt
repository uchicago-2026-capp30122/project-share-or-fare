For some of our graphs, we only display a sample of our dataset, to prevent overly long loading time and also to reduce visual clutter in the scatter plot. For all the histograms on the title page, as well as the scatter plot of Comparison of Public Tranportation and Rideshare Trip Times in tab 1, we are using a subset. The random subset is selected based on the last 1 or 2 digits of the Trip Time in Seconds column; We assume the last digits in trip seconds is random relativley to other factors we are analyzing, and so use that to select a random subset of 1/10th or 1/100th of our data to display in those charts.
